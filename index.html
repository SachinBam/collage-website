Computer Lab

Our computer lab is equipped with modern computers and high-speed internet, offering a space for students to improve their technical skills and enhance their learning experience.

Features

  • High-Speed Internet: Fast and reliable internet for all students to research and complete their assignments efficiently.
  • Advanced Computers: The lab is equipped with the latest computers that support all modern software and tools for learning.
  • Modern Software: Students have access to a wide range of software tools for educational purposes, including coding, design, and research applications.
  • 24/7 Access: The computer lab is open for students to use at any time, making it convenient for extra study sessions and practice.

Programming Languages

Students in our school have access to a variety of programming languages to learn and enhance their coding skills. Here are the languages taught in our computer lab:

  • Python: A powerful and versatile language used for web development, data science, machine learning, and more.
  • Java: A widely-used language for building applications, Android development, and enterprise software.
  • C++: A high-performance language used for system programming and game development.
  • JavaScript: A core technology for web development, used for building interactive and dynamic websites.
  • HTML/CSS: The building blocks of web design and development, used for creating and styling webpages.
  • PHP: A popular server-side scripting language for web development.

Modern Equipment

The lab is designed to provide the best learning experience with the latest technology and software tools available for students.
